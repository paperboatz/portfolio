Origin Story

The waft of AAA ribeye wasn’t strong enough to keep this gal in Cowtown/Calgary, Alberta. After building a creative foundation at Alberta College of Art and Design, she did what anyone would do… travel thousands of miles to Hamburg, Germany to study at Miami Ad School. There, she took her fill of bratwurst and schnitzel of advertising and wrangled her way into various agencies in London, San Fransisco, and NYC. Now, she’s in Toronto adding frontend development to her creative toolbox. Where to next? Who knows, but she’ll always be on the hunt for the meatiest projects.
